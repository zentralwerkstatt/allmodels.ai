To join the list please send a request with a personal introduction, possibly providing a reference to your work or webpage. Anonymous subscription is not possible.

Please note: Due to a high volume of requests, we are currently unable to confirm subscription requests immediately and kindly ask for your patience while we ensure that we are building a trustworthy community.

*

All Models is a research and discussion list conceived as an alternative to the fragmented communication and community building of social media. Nevertheless, feel free to share its content by any means: it is a platform for knowledge sharing and dissemination of critical discourse.

The list keeps very low and consistent traffic: avoid telegraphic replies, don't give notions for granted and provide context to your messages. Be clear: if you are replying to a particular post, note to whom you are replying and what it concerns. The scope of this mailing list is wide but not infinite. Please stay on topic.

Be mindful and respectful. Not everyone will agree during discussions. Please be aware that mails to this list will be distributed to many subscribers. Disrespectful behavior, spamming, and trolling will not be tolerated.

For privacy concerns, the list archive is available only to members.
